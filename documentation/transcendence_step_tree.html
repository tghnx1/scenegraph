How To Read It

One roadmap, two depths

The graph answers the roadmap question. The right-side panel answers the execution question. That way we keep the high-level picture visible without flattening all the details into one long list.

  • Step cards show the critical delivery levels.
  • Task cards show the branches inside the selected Step.
  • Task detail explains why the task exists, what it outputs, and what can go wrong.
